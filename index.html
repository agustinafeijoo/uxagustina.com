About me

Hi! I’m Agustina. Currently, I am a designer and partner at The UX Department, a digital agency with its HQ in SF.

I perform UX Research, interaction and visual design, and lead remote moderated usability testing sessions. I have participated in the creation and redesign of marketing websites, e-commerce websites, and both web and mobile applications.

My main drive is to focus on understanding the different user groups that a product will have in order to make informed design decisions and then validate those decisions through testing. The ultimate goal is to create simple and familiar interfaces that will solve a problem.

I have worked with world-class companies, from multiple countries including: 4INFO, Oaktree Capital Management, IdeaScale and their clients (including several government agencies, universities and Fortune 500 companies), VSP Global, Rockwell Collins, Vibrant America, Constellation Brands, Black Box Wines, ATT Monitor, Control Arms, JRK Property Holdings, and more.
